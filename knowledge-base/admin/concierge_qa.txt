CONCIERGE Q&A INTERACTION EXAMPLES
========================================

VISITOR IDENTIFICATION
----------------------
Question: "Hi, I'm here to see someone."
1. "Yeah I don't remember the apartment number... he's on like the 3rd or 4th floor."
   AI Response: "No problem. If you can share the resident's full name, I can look them up and notify them for you."
2. "I'm meeting my friend but I forgot his last name."
   AI Response: "That's okay. If you can provide the apartment number or full name, I can notify the resident for approval."
3. "She told me just to come by and the door would open."
   AI Response: "I'll still need to confirm the resident's name or apartment number so I can request approval for your visit."
4. "I'm not visiting, I'm just dropping something off real quick."
   AI Response: "Understood. If it's for a resident, I can notify them and guide you to the appropriate delivery area."
5. "I'm here for the guy with the dog... I think his name is Mike?"
   AI Response: "I'll need the resident's full name or apartment number to locate them and notify them for you."

VISITING A SPECIFIC RESIDENT
-----------------------------
Question: "I'm here to visit John Smith in apartment 412."
1. "Yeah I'm here for John... I think it's 412 or 421."
   AI Response: "Thanks. I'll notify John Smith in apartment 412 first. If that's not correct, we can try the other unit."
2. "John Smith's girlfriend told me to come."
   AI Response: "Thank you. I'll notify John Smith in apartment 412 to confirm your visit."
3. "I'm meeting John but he said he might not be home yet."
   AI Response: "I'll send John a notification to confirm your visit. Please hold while I wait for his response."
4. "John told me to just come up."
   AI Response: "I'll still need to confirm your visit with John before granting access. One moment while I notify him."
5. "Actually I'm meeting his roommate."
   AI Response: "No problem. I'll notify apartment 412 so the resident or roommate can approve your visit."

2. "It's for someone named Sarah... that's all I got."
   AI Response: "If you have the apartment number or full name, I can help notify the resident."
3. "I think it's supposed to go to the mailroom."

   AI Response: "I can attempt to notify the resident for you. If they do not respond, the groceries can be left in the delivery area."
